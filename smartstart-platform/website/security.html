Security Policy

Security first. Learn about AliceSolutionsGroup's security measures, incident response, and data protection practices.

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data and maintain the highest standards of information security.

2. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

All administrative accounts are protected with MFA. We enforce strong password policies and regular security updates.

3. Data Protection

We use encryption at rest and in transit, regular backups, and least-privilege access principles to protect your information.

4. Incident Response

In the event of a security incident, we have a documented response plan and will notify affected parties within 72 hours.

5. Compliance

Our security practices align with ISO 27001, SOC2, and other industry standards. We conduct regular security assessments.
