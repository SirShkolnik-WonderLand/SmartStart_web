About Udi Shkolnik

CISSP, CISM, ISO Lead Auditor, CTO, and venture builder with a mission to help people and businesses grow differently

Sagi Ehud "Udi" Shkolnik is the founder and driving force behind AliceSolutionsGroup and the visionary behind SmartStart, a platform and incubator designed to help people and businesses grow differently.

With a career built on cybersecurity leadership, operational restructuring, and innovation, Udi bridges the worlds of business, defense, and community. He is a CISSP, CISM, ISO 27001 Lead Auditor, DPO, and IT management executive, with certifications and experience that stretch across compliance, risk management, and cutting-edge automation.

Professional Journey

From defense contracts to venture building — a career at the intersection of technology and business

Cybersecurity Leadership

Designed and audited ISMS frameworks for enterprises (ISO 27001, SOC 2, NIST CSF, PHIPA, PIPEDA) across defense, healthcare, and global organizations.

Executive Roles

CTO and CISO across sectors, including healthcare, education, transportation, and logistics with cross-border experience in Israel, Canada, and the United States.

Military & Defense

Delivered NATO cyber training programs and military-grade resilience projects, including work with Plasan Sasa and national-level healthcare cybersecurity.

Restructuring & Innovation

At LGM Group, Udi cut inefficiencies, rebuilt processes, and saved $150K/month through smart automation and operational restructuring.

Venture Builder

Founded SmartStart, a structured incubator where SaaS products are built in 30 days with security and transparency at the core.

Education & Mentorship

Founded and led cybersecurity training school, delivering courses to children, professionals, and senior NATO officers on cybersecurity and GRC.

Community & Values

Beyond business — building a community around transparency, knowledge sharing, and growth

Udi's philosophy goes beyond business. His vision for AliceSolutionsGroup is about community, transparency, and shared growth.

Community Initiatives

  • Pro-Bono & Nonprofits: Offers free consulting and advisory work for nonprofits, schools, and community-driven organizations
  • Beer + Security Sessions: Hosts casual "Beer + Security" evenings across the GTA, where professionals gather to learn, share, and relax while discussing cyber and AI topics
  • Launch & Learn Workshops: Runs free workshops and knowledge-sharing events for founders, teams, and students to demystify technology and cybersecurity
  • Mentorship & Teaching: Draws on years of lecturing and training experience — from defense programs to executive boardrooms — to guide the next generation of builders and leaders

Core Beliefs

  • Security by Design — protect from day one, not later
  • Transparency Always — numbers and processes must be clear
  • Innovation Through Process — success is not about tools, but about understanding and improving workflows
  • Community First — knowledge should be shared, not hoarded

Impact & Recognition

Real results across industries and borders

Enterprise Impact

  • Plasan Sasa: Supported ISO/IEC 27001 certification with internal audits, risk treatment planning, and ISMS maturity improvements
  • Helen Doron Education Group: Guided a multi-country education network through ISO/IEC 27001 alignment, building consistent global security practices
  • Egged Transportation: Conducted ISO 27001 readiness and advisory to secure one of Israel's largest transportation companies
  • LGM Group: As CTO, led large-scale restructuring with automation of dispatch, sales, finance, and reporting, saving over $150K/month

National & International Projects

  • National Healthcare Projects (Israel): Designed and led cybersecurity & infrastructure hardening for a major hospital (500 doctors, 2,500+ staff). Supported the Ministry of Health in security and incident response initiatives
  • NATO Training Programs: Founded and led cybersecurity training programs, delivering courses to senior NATO officers at the Budapest Academy on cybersecurity, GRC, and compliance
  • Cybersecurity School & Training: Founded and led a cybersecurity training school, delivering courses to children, professionals, and senior leaders

Community Impact

  • Brought together dozens of professionals in the GTA through pro-bono work, Beer+Security meetups, and mentoring programs
  • Helped founders launch secure, sustainable SaaS ventures through SmartStart
  • Delivered multi-million-dollar classified projects across Israel and abroad
  • Cross-border operations experience in Israel, Canada, and United States
