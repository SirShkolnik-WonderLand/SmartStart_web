About Udi Shkolnik

CISSP, CISM, ISO Lead Auditor, CTO, and venture builder with a mission to help people and businesses grow differently

Sagi Ehud "Udi" Shkolnik is the founder and driving force behind AliceSolutionsGroup and the visionary behind SmartStart, a platform and incubator designed to help people and businesses grow differently.

With a career built on cybersecurity leadership, operational restructuring, and innovation, Udi bridges the worlds of business, defense, and community. He is a CISSP, CISM, ISO 27001 Lead Auditor, DPO, and IT management executive, with certifications and experience that stretch across compliance, risk management, and cutting-edge automation.

Professional Journey

From defense contracts to venture building — a career at the intersection of technology and business

Cybersecurity Leadership

Designed and audited ISMS frameworks for enterprises (ISO 27001, SOC 2, NIST CSF, PHIPA, PIPEDA) across defense, healthcare, and global organizations.

Executive Roles

CTO and CISO across sectors, including healthcare, education, transportation, and logistics with cross-border experience in Israel, Canada, and the United States.

Military & Defense

Delivered NATO cyber training programs and military-grade resilience projects, including work with Plasan Sasa and national-level healthcare cybersecurity.

Restructuring & Innovation

At LGM Group, Udi cut inefficiencies, rebuilt processes, and saved $150K/month through smart automation and operational restructuring.

Venture Builder

Founded SmartStart, a structured incubator where SaaS products are built in 30 days with security and transparency at the core.

Education & Mentorship

Founded and led cybersecurity training school, delivering courses to children, professionals, and senior NATO officers on cybersecurity and GRC.
