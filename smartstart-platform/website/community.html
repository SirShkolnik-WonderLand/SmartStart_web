Our Mission

What drives us to give back to the community

At AliceSolutionsGroup, we believe that security, structure, and transparency shouldn't be exclusive to large corporations. Our mission is to make professional-grade knowledge and tools accessible to everyone.

We're committed to supporting nonprofits, schools, startups, and community groups with the same level of expertise we provide to enterprise clients. This isn't about charity—it's about building a stronger, more secure community where everyone can thrive.

Whether you're a student learning about cybersecurity, a nonprofit protecting sensitive data, or a startup building your first security framework, we're here to help you grow differently.

Impact & Audience

Who we serve and how we make a difference

Who Benefits

Our community programs serve a diverse range of organizations and individuals:

  • Nonprofits protecting donor data and client information
  • Educational institutions securing student and research data
  • Startups and small businesses building their first security frameworks
  • Community organizations needing guidance on digital best practices
  • Students and emerging professionals looking to learn from real-world experience

Where We Serve

While we're primarily active in the Greater Toronto Area, our virtual sessions allow us to support organizations globally. We believe that good security practices and business knowledge shouldn't be limited by geography.
