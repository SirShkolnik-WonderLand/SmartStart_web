Understanding North York's Business Landscape

Real data and insights about North York's unique cybersecurity challenges

North York is one of Toronto's six administrative districts, home to approximately 683,511 people across 176.87 km² (population density: 3,864.5 per km²). The area features North York City Centre, a high-density business district with numerous office towers along Yonge Street, plus major retail centers like Yorkdale Mall and Fairview Mall.

The healthcare sector is particularly prominent, with North York General Hospital and numerous medical clinics and offices throughout the area. This concentration of healthcare facilities creates specific compliance requirements around patient data protection and PHIPA regulations.

North York's excellent connectivity—served by TTC subway lines (Line 1 Yonge-University, Line 4 Sheppard) and major highways (401, 404, Highway 400)—makes it a hub for businesses that need reliable, secure infrastructure.

Business Density
