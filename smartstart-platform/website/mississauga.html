Why Mississauga (Real Facts)

Explosive Software Growth

Over the last five years, Mississauga's software sector employment grew ~138%, the fastest among GTA cities. This growth sits within a region that's among North America's fastest-growing tech talent hubs.

Source: Invest Mississauga

Global Corporate Base

Mississauga consistently ranks among Canada's top locations for business and investment; founders can access programs and mentors at IDEA Mississauga at Square One (a 4,300-sq-ft entrepreneurship hub).

Source: City of Mississauga

Jobs & Capacity

City employment data shows ~501,500 employees in 2024 and +74,200 jobs over 10 years—evidence of sustained economic depth to support scale-ups and supplier ecosystems.

Source: City of Mississauga

Connectivity & Transit

The Hazel McCallion (Hurontario) LRT—an 18 km line with 19 stations—continues construction between Port Credit and Brampton Gateway; the Mississauga Transitway (BRT) provides an 18 km rapid bus corridor.

Source: Wikipedia - Hurontario LRT
