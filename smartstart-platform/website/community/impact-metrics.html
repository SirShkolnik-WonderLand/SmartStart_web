Our Mission

SmartStart Community operates as a non-profit initiative of AliceSolutions Inc., dedicated to supporting entrepreneurs, students, and small businesses in Ontario by providing access to mentorship, training, and resources.

🏢 Entrepreneur Support

Free and low-cost initiatives including startup clinics, mentorship sessions, and pro-bono cybersecurity consulting for local entrepreneurs.

🎓 Education & Training

Cybersecurity awareness training, business development workshops, and skill-building programs for students and professionals.

🤝 Community Building

Regular networking events, knowledge-sharing sessions, and collaborative programs that connect entrepreneurs across the GTA.
