Event Policies

What to expect and how we ensure a positive experience for everyone

🎫 Registration

  • RSVP required for all events
  • Registration opens 2-4 weeks before event
  • Waitlist available for popular events
  • Last-minute cancellations accepted

💰 Cost

  • All community events are free
  • Participants pay for their own food/drinks
  • No hidden fees or upsells
  • Venue partnerships keep costs low

📝 Code of Conduct

  • Respectful and inclusive environment
  • No harassment or discrimination
  • Professional networking focus
  • Report issues to event organizers

♿ Accessibility

  • Wheelchair accessible venues
  • Virtual options for most events
  • Accommodation requests welcome
  • Contact us for specific needs
