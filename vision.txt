### **Core Principles**
1. **Equity is Earned, Not Given**: Ownership based on actual value delivered
2. **Founder Protection**: Minimum 35% equity protection for project originators
3. **Fair Distribution**: AliceSolutions maximum 25% for infrastructure and support
4. **Dynamic Allocation**: Contributors earn 0.5% - 5% per contribution
5. **Transparency**: All equity decisions are visible and auditable

### **Business Rules**
- **Project Owner**: Minimum 35% equity (protected from dilution)
- **AliceSolutions**: Maximum 25% equity (infrastructure, tools, legal support)
- **Contributors**: 0.5% - 5% per contribution (based on effort, impact, complexity)
- **Reserve Pool**: 15-40% for future investors and growth
